UN SDG 12
Responsible Consumption and Production


Dhrubo Paul




In this project, I am presenting an immersive experience where people will at first go through the current habit of food and clothing consumption. We will see piles of clothes, and abundance of fast food.

Then the viewers will go through the effects of such habits. We will see lots of garbage everywhere, which resembles the earth we are going through right now.

After looking at this future, viewer will take another turn. They can see logo of our organization: The Story of Stuff Project. They will then enter the new habits and respective accelerated future.

Here we will see the habit of slow clothing and slow food, which will increase awareness about responsible consumption and production. The story ends with a view of a sustainable environment.
